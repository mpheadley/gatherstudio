Congratulations — your new website is live! Here are 3 things you can do today to help Google find your site faster and start getting you calls.

---

### 1. Share It on Social Media (5 minutes)

Post your new website link on:

- [ ] Your **personal Facebook** — "Excited to announce our new website! Check it out: [URL]"
- [ ] Your **business Facebook page** (if you have one)
- [ ] Any **local community groups** you're in (e.g., "[City] Word of Mouth", "[County] Buy/Sell/Trade", neighborhood groups)
- [ ] **Instagram** — post a screenshot of the homepage with the link in your bio

**Why this matters:** Google sees a spike in traffic from social media and treats it as a signal that your site is active and relevant. More early visits = faster indexing.

**Sample post you can copy/paste:**
> "We just launched our new website! If you need [SERVICE] in [CITY], check us out at [URL]. We'd love to hear what you think!"

---

### 2. Ask 2-3 Past Customers for a Google Review (5 minutes)

Text or message 2-3 happy customers and ask them to leave a quick Google review.

**Sample text you can send:**
> "Hey [NAME]! We just launched a new website and we're trying to build up our Google presence. Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It'd mean a lot. Here's the link: [GOOGLE_REVIEW_LINK]"

**Why this matters:** Google sees new reviews + a new website and "promotes" your listing in local search results. Even 2-3 reviews in the first week make a big difference.

**How to get your Google Review link:**
1. Search for your business name on Google
2. Click "Write a review" on your Business Profile
3. Copy the URL from your browser — that's your review link
4. (Or: I can send you the direct link if you'd like)

---

### 3. Update Your Links Everywhere (5 minutes)

Make sure your new website URL is in all the places people find you:

- [ ] **Google Business Profile** — update your website link (I may have already done this for you)
- [ ] **Facebook page** — update the website field in your page info
- [ ] **Instagram bio** — update the link
- [ ] **Email signature** — add your website URL
- [ ] **Any directories** you're listed on (Yelp, Angi, HomeAdvisor, etc.)
- [ ] **Business cards** — order new ones with the URL if needed
